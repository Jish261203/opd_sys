System Workflow
  1. Create Patient: Register a new patient with name, gender, age, and phone number.
  2. Book Appointment: Schedule an appointment for an active patient with a doctor.
  3. Record Consultation: When the patient arrives, create a consultation record with vitals and notes.
  4. Mark Complete: Once consultation is done, mark it as completed. This automatically completes the appointment.
  5. View History: Access completed consultations for any patient.
